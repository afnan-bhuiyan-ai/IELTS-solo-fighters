What Band 8–9 scorers actually did

⏱
45–90 min/day

Top scorers never grinded 5-hour sessions. Short, focused, daily practice beats marathon studying.

🎯
Task types first

They studied scoring criteria and question formats — not "general English." Know the exam inside out.

🎙
Record & review

Every high scorer recorded their speaking, listened back, and fixed filler words and pacing.

📝
Mock test every 2 weeks

Full timed tests under real conditions. No shortcuts. Familiarity removes exam-day panic.

🔍
Reading = information finding

Band 9 scorers never read everything. They read questions first, then scan for answers.
